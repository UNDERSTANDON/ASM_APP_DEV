# Overview: 
BrightPath Learning is a small development team with limited experience in mobile application development. They are building a mobile app called “AI Study Mentor”, designed exclusively for students at middle school, high school, and university levels.
The core purpose of Study Mentor is to provide an AI-powered question-and-answer learning platform, where students can directly ask academic questions and receive instant, intelligent answers from AI.
 There are no teachers or lecturers involved in the system. All explanations, solutions, and learning guidance are generated automatically by AI.
The application focuses on on-demand learning, allowing students to solve homework problems, clarify concepts, and improve understanding anytime, anywhere.

# Functional requirements:

1. Account Auth:

	2. Account required to have: "Question history", "Saved Answers", "Learning stat" (optional)

		+ Education levels: enum "Middle school", "Highschool", "Colege", "University".
		+ Majors / subjects

2. AI question submission:
	1. Submissions:

	2. Major coverage:
		+ Science
		+ Mathematic
		+ Programming
		+ History

3. AI Response generation:
